# Behind the Scenes — How We Help CEOs Sleep Better at Night

## Idea
**Hook**: Behind the scenes: How we help CEOs sleep better at night.

**Language**: EN

**Theme**: Experience / Brand Story

**Angle**: Soft sell — show what it's like to work with us, build trust, humanize the brand.

**Talking Points**:
- The reality: CEOs lose sleep over people, cash flow, and the feeling that everything depends on them — not strategy.
- We don't open with a deck — we open with three questions that surface the REAL problem, often not the one we were hired for.
- 3 months later: fewer hero decisions, more system. CEO finally rests.

> **Recorded**: `[ ] yes` — Date:

**Length target**: ~45 seconds (~115 words)

```
[HOOK — 5s]
Behind the scenes: how we help CEOs sleep better at night.

[BODY — 35s]
Most CEOs don't lose sleep over strategy. They lose sleep over people, cash flow, and the feeling that everything depends on them.
So when we walk in, we don't start with a deck. We start with three questions.
What's keeping you up at night? What's your team afraid to tell you? Where is the cash actually going?
Then we map the real problem — usually not the one we were hired for — and rebuild the system around it. Roles. Rhythms. Numbers. Decisions.
Three months later, the CEO is making fewer decisions and the business runs better. That's the goal. Less hero, more system.

[CTA — 5s]
Sleeping with your phone on the pillow isn't a strategy. DM us 'SLEEP' and let's talk.
```
