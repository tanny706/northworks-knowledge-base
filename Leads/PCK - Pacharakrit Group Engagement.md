## Client Background — Pacharakrit Group

### What they actually do
Pacharakrit Group is a **Fund Management** house — manages capital for global Private Equity, Credit Funds and Family Offices across Singapore, Hong Kong, UK, US, Qatar. Acts as Thai partner to **Temasek, DBS Bank, Blackstone, Apollo, QIA**.

**Credit portfolio scale**: 3,326M THB (2022) → projected **33,789M THB (2025)** — ~10x growth in 3 years.

### Group structure (6 subsidiaries)
1. **Royal Global Property** — Chiang Rai-focused real estate (highrise condo opposite Mae Fah Luang U, detached houses, sports community mall)
2. **Royal Global Hospitality** — 4–5 hotels in development with **Pullman, Mercure, Courtyard by Marriott**
3. **WP & PC Development (Sport City)** — FIFA-partnered sport city; only FIFA-standard stadium in Thailand; plans to bring David Beckham to launch
4. **S.P. Leasing Guarantee** — 40-year-old auto loan / guarantee business; ~500M THB co-investment in "Muang Thong Auto Racing"; ~500M THB performing loan book
5. **Pacharakrit Wealth** — wealth management + VC/Startup investing (Jaymart exec network; ~20M THB deployed early, some losses)
6. **The Key** — JV property management; luxury villas at Kamala Beach Phuket (500M THB/unit), 20-rai Wellness Hotel, Chao Phraya riverside hotel

### Property Lender & Finance services
Structured Finance, Project Finance, Equity Financing, JV co-investment, Factoring, Stock Loan, Government Contract-Secured Lending, Mortgage / Sale-with-Right-of-Redemption, 35-year residential loans.

### Client / project roster (proof of scale)
Backer for **The Forestias (MQDC), Ideo (Ananda), CMC, Origin, Charn Issara**. Government work: Department of Corrections solar project, Royal Thai Air Force drone procurement.

**Implication for us:** This is a serious group. Engagement quality and discretion matter — they back top-tier developers. Get this right and the reference flow is significant.

## Opportunity

### Khun Nut's 5 strategic priorities (verbatim from meeting)
1. **Rebuild the parent company first** — Pacharakrit Group HQ is "the heart"; even though subsidiaries are doing well, parent is down to 5 people and must be restored before expanding consulting to the subsidiaries.
2. **Lay full-stack HR foundation** — Vision, Mission, Brand Positioning, Org Chart, headcount plan, HR Roadmap. End-to-end, not piecemeal.
3. **Ownership Mindset culture** — explicitly modeled on MLM / insurance company cultures (Amway, Legacy-style resilience). Wants employees who feel like owners, can handle rejection, and aren't waiting for instructions ("rote work can be done by AI").
4. **Professional recruitment & screening** — especially RMs (Relationship Manager / Credit Officers) and Leader-level hires. Wants an expert (us) to screen for attitude/mindset fit, not just CV match.
5. **Training & Development system** — intensive onboarding that codifies mindset + working style + product knowledge. Open to throwing out existing JDs / KPIs / Workflows and rebuilding.

### Three pain points / blind spots
- **Staff exodus crisis**: 25–30 → 5 people. Workload now unsustainable despite subsidiaries running fine.
- **Past MD problems** (both failed):
  - 1st MD (female): created drama and internal division.
  - 2nd MD (male, poached from True): hit revenue targets but verbally abusive; entire team quit in December.
- **CEO's own admission — "too kind" management**: Refuses to issue written warning letters (feels like "a court summons"); uses verbal warnings and "the cane" (mai-riao / ไม้เรียว) — neither solves the problem.
- **Self-aware blind spot**: "I don't know what I don't know" about why he can't retain talent or scale the org.

### What they want from us (the culture target)
An organization where employees are loyal, faithful, and have MLM/insurance-grade mindset strength — able to absorb rejection from clients and keep going.
