## Our methodology

```
DIAGNOSE   →   DESIGN   →   DEPLOY   →   EMBED
(2–3 wks)      (3–6 wks)    (4–12 wks)   (ongoing / retainer)
```

**Diagnose** — interview founder + key staff, audit current docs/practice, map risks (legal + cultural + structural)

**Design** — recommend structure (org, grades, policy, comp); founder signs off ก่อน execute

**Deploy** — produce artifacts จริง (contracts, JDs, letters, calculators); train internal team; pilot ก่อน rollout

**Embed** — retainer model สำหรับ founder advisory + edge cases + quarterly review

**Principle:** เริ่มจาก minimum viable ที่ scale ได้ — ไม่ over-engineer

## Thai labor law — non-negotiable depth

**คู่แข่งหลายรายอ้างว่าทำได้ — Northworks ทำได้จริง:**

- **Labour Protection Act 2541** + 2562 + 2566 amendments, cited by section number
- **Severance schedule (มาตรา 118)** — 30/90/180/240/300/400 days, base salary definition (รวม regular allowances)
- **Termination for cause (มาตรา 119)** — 6 grounds + evidentiary standard; ป้องกัน "unfair termination" claim
- **Probation 119-day rule** — defensible probation termination
- **DLPW enforcement practice** — what triggers complaint, how local labor office settles vs. court
- **Social Security, Provident Fund, PDPA** — applied, not theoretical
- **Internal Work Rules (ข้อบังคับเกี่ยวกับการทำงาน)** — mandatory at 10+ staff, foundation of disciplinary defensibility

เมื่อ stake สูง — Northworks recommend Thai labor lawyer และ tell founder ว่าต้องถามอะไร

**Client profile:** Thai financial services group, multi-entity, backed by institutional investors
**Challenge:** Build frontline RM + Team Leader compensation structure to compete directly with market leader, without overpaying

## Working principles

**1. Just-in-time, not just-in-case.** Build what you need at headcount today + next 12 months — ไม่ใช่ระบบที่ enterprise consultants อยากขาย

**2. Founder respects time.** Lead with the answer; depth on request. CEO meetings end with decisions, not homework

**3. Confidentiality is operational, not optional.** Names, salaries, founder doubts — held like a lawyer or priest would

**4. Push back when stakes are high.** If a decision is illegal, ethically wrong, or financially reckless — เราพูดตรง, professionally

**5. Right-sized always.** บริษัท 30 คนไม่ต้องการ 9-box grid; parent-co 5 คนไม่ต้องการ HRIS

**6. Deliver artifacts, not advice.** Letter drafted, calculation done, JD ready — copy-paste usable
